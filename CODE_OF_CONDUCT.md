# Code of conduct

## Standard

Mallard adopts the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

In short: be respectful, assume good intent, focus on the work. Personal attacks, harassment, and bad-faith engagement are not welcome.

## Enforcement

Report violations to the repository owner via GitHub. Reports are handled privately. The owner may warn, temporarily ban, or permanently ban contributors who repeatedly violate the standard.

The full Contributor Covenant text governs this project verbatim.
